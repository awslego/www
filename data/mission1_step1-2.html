Check your Local network

Cheat Sheet

1. (Terminal #2) Check your Local network
>  eth.accounts;      // We have already created one account for quick mining. Check it out
>  eth.getBalance(eth.accounts[0]);
>  eth.blockNumber;

>  miner.start();      // Wait until you see the successful message on Terminer #1
>  miner.stop();

>  eth.getBalance(eth.accounts[0]);
>  web3.fromWei(eth.getBalance(eth.accounts[0]), 'ether');      // Change Wei to ETH (1Wei = 10^18 ETH)
>  eth.blockNumber;

2. (Terminal #2) Create an account
>  personal.newAccount('1');        // '1' is this account's passphrase
>  eth.accounts;

    (Terminal #3) Remove an account
  cd ~/mission1/local-data; ls -al
  cd keystore; ls -al
  rm [2nd file]

3. (Terminal #3) Check your final account
>  eth.accounts;
