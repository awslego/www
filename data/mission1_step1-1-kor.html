SSH 접속하기

Cheat Sheet

✪ Mac용

1. chmod 400 [path]/[key-pair.pem]

2. ssh -i [path]/[key-pair.pem] ec2-user@[public_IP]



✪ Window용

1. PuTTY를 설치하세요. https://the.earth.li/~sgtatham/putty/latest/w64/putty-64bit-0.70-installer.msi(64bit)

2. puTTYgen를 설치하세요. https://the.earth.li/~sgtatham/putty/latest/w64/puttygen.exe (64bit)

3. PuTTYgen을 실행해서 키 인증서를 PPK형식으로 변환하세요.
