Check the Private Network

Cheat Sheet

1. (Terminal #2) Check the Private Network

>  eth.blockNumber;
>  eth.accounts;

>  net.peerCount;      ⇐ Peer's Count
>  admin.peers;         ⇐ Peer's Info.

>  eth.getBlock(0);      ⇐ Genesis Block Number
>  eth.getBlock(31);      ⇐ Block number containing 'myFirst' Contract in the private network

> eth.getTransaction(eth.getBlock(31).transactions[0]);                 ⇐ 'myFirst' Contract Transaction Info.
> eth.getTransactionReceipt(eth.getBlock(31).transactions[0]);      ⇐ 'myFirst' Contract TransactionReceipt Info.
