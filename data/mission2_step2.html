Exercise Geth Console

Cheat Sheet

1. Terminal #2

[ec2-user@node1] ~/mission2> ./gethconsole
>  eth.blockNumber;
>  eth.accounts;

>  net.peerCount;      ⇐ Peer's Count
>  admin.peers;         ⇐ Peer's Info.

>  eth.getBlock(0);      ⇐ Get Genesis Block
>  eth.getBlock(11);      ⇐ Get Token Contract
>  eth.getBlock(15);      ⇐ Get Crowdfunding Contract

> eth.getTransaction(eth.getBlock(15).transactions[0]);                 ⇐ Get Transaction
> eth.getTransactionReceipt(eth.getBlock(15).transactions[0]);      ⇐ Get TransactionReceipt
